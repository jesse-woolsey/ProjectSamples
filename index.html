Featured Launch 1

Scenario Based Learning: An Instructional Design Process Full Walk-Through

Overview + Purpose: Following the ADDIE Method, this eLearning experience was designed and developed for use within a sales workforce to solve the problem that stress and emotional responses negatively impact performance and ability to meet performance targets. The learning solution solves this problem by equipping sales reps with breathing exercises for self-regulation during demanding sales calls and workdays.

  • Audience: Sales Workforce

  • Responsibilities: Action Mapping, Brainstorming learning solutions, Instructional Design, eLearning Development, Visual Design, Storyboard, Mockups, Data Analysis

  • Tools Used: Articulate Storyline, Mural, Miro, Google Docs, Photopea, GitHub Pages, TalentLMS, and Survey Monkey with open-source resources from Freepik, Pixabay, and flaticon

  • Process: See below for steps, explanations, and samples tracking the ID process through the ADDIE Method

  • Final Product: Click "NEXT" below to begin the ‚ÄúBreath/Work" eLearning Experience in a new tab

ANALYSIS STAGE - Action Mapping in Mural:

  • Process: I imagined inviting key stakeholders from the client‚Äôs organization to investigate the question: ‚ÄúHow can we achieve workplace well-being through elimination of anxiety and stress during sales work?‚Äù Leveraging research and experience in addition to collaborative insights from imagined subject matter experts and sales supervisors, I created an ACTION MAP outlining the specific actions workers need to take during sales work and associated stress and emotions that need to be managed. I then brainstormed and proposed learning solutions, which obtained approval before imitating design work.

  • Tool Used: Mural

  • Sample: Action Map and Learning Solution Proposal



DESIGN STAGE: Text-Based Storyboard and Collaborative Visual Style Guide:

  • Process: After I built a Visual Style Guide, stakeholders were invited to provide feedback. Collaboratively, the team made final design choices by using digital Post-Its as a voting tool.

  • Tools Used: Miro and free-license design files Freepik, Pixabay, Coolors, Flaticon

  • Sample: Collaborative Visual Style GUide

  • Process: A Text-Based Storyboarding was created based on approved content and establishes a character-driven, scenario-based learning experience. Learner actions and practice scenarios reflect requirements determined through previous Action Mapping. Triggers, layer cues, and branching logic were also established to minimize issues during the development phase.

  • Tool Used: Google Docs

  • Sample: Text-Based Storyboard

DEVELOPMENT STAGE - Full development in Articulate Storyline with illustration work in Photopea:

  • Process: Work started with creating custom Master Slides with collaboratively chosen color palette, font styles, and button design. The development process in Storyline utilizing triggers, item states, variables, slide layers, and branching scenarios to allow learners the ability to progress through experience based on their own choices. Learners receive immediate, real-world feedback during Scenario Based Quiz questions. Any additional illustration work for character or design customization was completed through the web-based editing tool Photopea.

  • Tool Used: Articulate Storyline

  • Sample 1: Storyline triggers, default states, and variables were used to progress learners through content and represent progress.

  • Sample 2: Timed triggers, item animations, and slide layers were utilized to provide animated visual experience for breathing exercises. Branching scenarios allow a learner to repeat breathing exercises as desired.

  • Sample 3: Based on scenario-based responses, learners receive immediate positive or negative real-world feedback. Additionally, a marker was used as a hidden, optional hint tool.

  • Sample 4: A branching scenario was established utilizing quiz results and slide layers to provide customized learning content.

  • Sample 5: Customized Design and Illustration Work in Photopea

IMPLEMENT & EVALUATE STAGES - Uploaded to TalentLMS, Assign to reps, and Automate 30-Day Survey

  • Process: After uploading the course to TalentLMS, Breath/Work was assigned to all imagined sales representatives. Reps were given a week to complete the course. Upon course completion, management receives reporting on pass/fail rates to allow for additional coaching or support. Additionally, the TalentLMS automates sending a 30-Day follow up feedback and course evaluation survey created in Survey Monkey. This survey provides an evaluation of the course as well as insight into content effectiveness in the workplace and a chance to share ideas to improve future training experiences.

  • Tool Used: TalentLMS

  • Sample: Course Created in TalentLMS. Assigned users automatically receive a feedback and evaluation survey upon course completion.

  • Tool Used: Survey Monkey

  • Sample: 30-Day Evaluation & Feedback Survey

Rapid Deployment with AI and a Brand Voice in Articulate Rise

Overview + Purpose: This eLearning design experience was built with the purpose of leveraging AI tools for rapid deployment within Articulate Rise with the added challenge of utilizing a brand voice.

  • Audience: Users in the market for a new kayak

  • Responsibilities: Brainstorming content subject, crafting AI prompts for text content, building AI prompts for illustration and design work, and rapid development in Articulte Rise

  • Tools Used: Articulate Rise, ChatGPT, Copy AI, Neural.Love AI, NightCafe, and Articulate Content Library

  • Process: I prompted ChatGPT to describe Bob Ross‚Äôs brand voice. Then, that description was used to establish a Bob Ross brand voice within Copy AI. Using interactive and evolving prompts, Copy AI was then used to create a course outline, content, and finally scenario-based assessment. Illustrations for the course were created to mirror Bob Ross paintings using generative prompts in Neural.Love AI and NightCafe. Finally, Articulate Rise was used for rapid development, including utilization of the scenario block feature to engage the learner in the application of content covered.

  • Final Product: Click "Start Course" below to begin "A Guide To Kayaks: AI-Bob Ross Edition" in a new tab

  • Sample 1: The AI tool ChatGPT was used to rapidly create a brand voice, specifically that of Bob Ross

  • Sample 2: The AI tool Copy AI generated scenario based assessment questions, which were based of the previously AI-generated content developed through prompt iterations and evolutions

  • Sample 3: Using incrementally evolving prompts, Neutral.Love AI created a variety of illustrations for use in the eLearning experience. The prompts were written to generate illustrations mirroring the "wet-on-wet" oil painting style of Bob Ross

  • Sample 4: The image-based palette tool on Coloor was used to determine a custom color for development in Articulate Rise. The image being used was created using the AI illustration tool Neutral.Love AI.
